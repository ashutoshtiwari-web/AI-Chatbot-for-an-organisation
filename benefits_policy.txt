COMPANY HR POLICY DOCUMENT — BENEFITS

Health Insurance:
- Company offers PPO and HMO plans through Blue Shield
- Company covers 80% of employee premiums; employee pays 20%
- Dependents can be added during open enrollment or within 30 days of a qualifying life event
- Coverage begins on the first day of employment

Dental & Vision:
- Dental: Delta Dental Basic (preventive 100% covered, basic 80%, major 50%)
- Vision: VSP Standard (annual eye exam covered, $150 frame allowance)

401k Retirement Plan:
- Employees are eligible to contribute immediately upon hire
- Company matches 100% of contributions up to 3% of salary
- Vesting schedule: 25% per year, fully vested after 4 years
- Annual IRS contribution limit applies

Open Enrollment:
- Held annually November 1–30
- Changes take effect January 1 of the following year
- Qualifying life events (marriage, birth, etc.) allow mid-year changes

FSA / HSA:
- Health FSA available for PPO members: up to $3,050/year pre-tax
- HSA available for HDHP members; company contributes $500/year

Life Insurance:
- Basic life insurance: 2x annual salary, company-paid
- Supplemental life insurance available up to 5x salary

Employee Assistance Program (EAP):
- Free confidential counseling: up to 8 sessions/year
- Access 24/7 via 1-800-EAP-HELP
