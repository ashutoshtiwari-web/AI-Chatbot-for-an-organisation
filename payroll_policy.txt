COMPANY HR POLICY DOCUMENT — PAYROLL

Pay Schedule:
- All employees are paid on a bi-weekly basis (every 2 weeks, 26 pay periods per year)
- Pay dates fall on the 1st and 15th of every month
- Direct deposit is processed 1 business day before the pay date

Deduction Types:
- Federal Income Tax: withheld based on W-4 elections
- State Income Tax: varies by state of residence
- Social Security: 6.2% of gross wages (up to annual cap)
- Medicare: 1.45% of gross wages
- 401k: employee-elected percentage (pre-tax)
- Health Insurance Premiums: deducted pre-tax
- Dental & Vision Premiums: deducted pre-tax

Pay Stubs:
- Electronic pay stubs are available on the HR portal under "My Pay"
- Paper copies available on request from HR

Overtime:
- Non-exempt employees are eligible for 1.5x pay for hours worked beyond 40/week
- Overtime must be pre-approved by a manager

Payroll Corrections:
- Report errors within 5 business days to payroll@company.com
- Corrections are processed in the next pay cycle
